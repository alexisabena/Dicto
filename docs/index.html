Core capabilities

Everything your practice dictates

01 / Recording

Session recording + diarization

Open the web app on any phone or desktop. Press Start. Dicto records up to an hour of consultation audio, separates up to five speakers — doctor, nurse, patient, family — and labels each one by name after a one-time 30-second voice enrollment.

02 / Transcription

Spanish-first medical transcription

Every chunk of audio is sent to OpenAI Whisper the moment it lands. The result is a timestamped, speaker-tagged Markdown file saved to the patient folder — readable in any editor, Obsidian-compatible, zero proprietary lock-in.

03 / Scheduling

WhatsApp appointment booking

Patients message your clinic number. Dicto's conversation agent — defined in plain YAML, customizable without code — reads your calendar, proposes slots, and books the appointment. Confirmation goes to the patient and to you. No receptionist required.

04 / Insurance

Auto-filled insurance claim PDFs

After a procedure, answer guided voice questions about diagnosis, materials, and complications. Dicto extracts the structured fields, maps them to the correct insurer form (IMSS, ISSSTE, GNP, AXA, MetLife, and more), and generates a draft PDF for your review in under two minutes.

05 / Patient Files

Structured patient folders

Each patient gets a dedicated folder: demographics, consultation notes, procedure records, claims, and raw audio — all linked by wikilinks. Open the folder in Obsidian and your practice becomes a visual knowledge graph. No proprietary database needed.

06 / Cost

Designed to be affordable

Dicto runs on a single $9 VPS and costs roughly $24/mo at MVP scale. Whisper is billed at $0.006 per minute of audio. There are no per-seat fees, no VC-backed pricing surprises. One paying clinic covers the entire infrastructure.

How it works

Three steps. Zero paperwork.

01

You speak. Dicto listens.

Press Start on any device. The session records continuously, auto-pausing in silence and resuming when you speak. All audio is chunked and uploaded in real time — if you lose connection, nothing is lost.

02

Transcription and labeling happen automatically.

Each audio chunk goes through Whisper for transcription and pyannote for speaker diarization. The output is a clean, speaker-labeled Markdown file in the patient's folder within minutes of the session ending.

03

Records and claims are filed.

Consultation notes are saved. If it was a procedure, a guided Q&A fills the insurance claim form. If the patient needs a follow-up, WhatsApp handles the booking. You sign off, Dicto files.
